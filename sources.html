Data Source

The sole data source used while completing this project was the U.S. Census Bureau's American FactFinder. American FactFinder (AFF) is one of the primary sites that the Bureau utilizes for disseminating the nation's statistics. Data items such as General Housing Characteristics, Annual Population Estimates, Housing Estimates, Economic Statistics, and more can be found on AFF. The specific survey that was used was the Annual Survey of Manufactures (ASM). The ASM is a yearly survey that is mailed to thousands of manufacturing businesses, and collects a variety of data such as receipts, capital expenditures, benefits, and inventories. My analysis in this project pertains to value of shipments/receipts, cost of materials, annual payroll, and employment.
